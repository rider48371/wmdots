FLUXBOX KEY BINDINGS
----------------
Super + Return --- Launch Tilix
Super + q --- Close window
Super + Space --- Launch XFCE4 Appfinder
Super + Right --- Tile 50% Right
Super + Left --- Tile 50% Left
Super + Up --- Maximize
Super + Down --- Resize and Move Center
Super + Ctrl + Left --- Resize Left 
Super + Ctrl + Right --- Resize Right
Super + Shift + n --- Toggle WindowFollow
Super + Shift + Right --- Move Window to Next Tag
Super + Shift + Left --- Move Window to Prev Tag
Super + Tab --- Cycle Applications
Super + Shift + Tab --- Cycle Prev Applications
Super + Shift + q --- Quit Fluxbox
Super + Shift + r --- Restart Fluxbox

FLUXBOX TAG BINDINGS
----------------
Super + (1-9,0) --- Switch to tag (workspace 1-10)
Super + Shift + (1-9,0) ---  Move Window to tag (workspace 1-10)
Super + Ctrl + (1-9,0) ---  Move and Focus Window on tag (workspace 1-10)

APPLICATION KEY BINDINGS
------------------
Super + Space --- XFCE4-Appfinder
Super + Return --- Tilix
Super + b --- Firefox-ESR
Super + Shift + b --- Firefox-ESR -private-window
Super + f --- Thunar
Super + e --- Geany
Super + v --- Pulsemixer

AUDIO
-----
Super + Insert --- Volume Up
Super + Delete --- Volume Down
Super + m --- Muted

SCROT
-----
Super + @Print --- Select Rectangle Screenshot
@Print --- Fullscreen Screenshot

REDSHIFT
--------
Super + Alt + r --- Redshift On (night)
Super + Alt + b --- Redshift off (daytime)
