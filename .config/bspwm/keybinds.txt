BSPWM KEY BINDINGS
------------------
Super + x --- Clipmenu
Super + Shift + c --- Close window
Super + Right --- Change Focus Right
Super + Left --- Change Focus Left
Super + Up --- Change Focus Up
Super + Down --- Change Focus Down
Super + Shift + Right --- Move Window Right
Super + Shift + Left --- Move Window Left
Super + Shift + Up --- Move Window Up
Super + Shift + Down --- Move Window Down
Super + Ctrl + Left --- Resize Left 
Super + Ctrl + Right --- Resize Right
Super + Ctrl + Up --- Resize Up 
Super + Ctrl + Down --- Resize Down

Super + Shift + q --- Powermenu
Super + Shift + r --- Restart BSPWM
Super + ESC --- Restart SXHKD
Super + h --- This screen

ROFI MENUS
----------
Super + Shift + Return --- Rofi Menu
Super + b --- Rofi Bluetooth
Super + Shift + e --- Rofi Emoji Picker
Super + Shift + w --- Rofi Wifi Picker
Super + Shift + x --- Rofi Calculator
BSPWM TAG BINDINGS
----------------
Super + (1-9,0) --- Switch to tag (workspace 1-10)
Super + Shift + (1-9,0) ---  Move and Focus Window to tag (workspace 1-10)
	
SXHKD KEY BINDINGS
------------------
Super + Return --- Alacritty
Super + Shift + b --- Google-Chrome
Super + Shift + f --- Ranger
Super + v --- Pulsemixer

AUDIO
-----
Super + Insert --- Volume Up
Super + Delete --- Volume Down
Super + m --- Muted

SCROT
-----
Super + @Print --- Select Rectangle Screenshot
@Print --- Fullscreen Screenshot
